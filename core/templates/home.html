Key Features

Everything you need to manage student activities efficiently

Role-Based Access

Separate dashboards for students and faculty with role-based permissions. Students submit activities, faculty review and approve them.

Easy Submission

Students can easily submit activities with certificates. Support for PDF and image files with a simple upload interface.

Activity Tracking

Track activity status in real-time. View pending, approved, and rejected activities with detailed information.

Smart Filtering

Faculty can filter activities by status and category for efficient review and management.

Activity Reports

Generate and view comprehensive activity reports. Print-friendly format for official documentation.

Secure & Reliable

Built with Django framework for security and reliability. All data is securely stored and managed.

How It Works

Simple steps to manage your activities

1 For Students
  1. Register and create your student profile
  2. Submit activities with event details and certificates
  3. Track the status of your submissions
  4. View your approved activities report
2 For Faculty
  1. Register and login as faculty
  2. View all activity submissions
  3. Review and approve or reject activities
  4. Filter and manage activities efficiently
